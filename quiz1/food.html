My Local Foods

Delicous and Unique foods
from my hometown

Surabaya is home of many delicious and unique culiners that you can't find it anywhere else in the world.

In here, the peoples likes peanut sauce and shrip space, so be prepared to taste a lot of it on dishes in Surabaya :)

Here's my top picks for Local Culiner in Surabaya that I think you guys should try!!

Tahu Campur

This dished is popular for its bumbu petis which is shirp paste. This dishes consist of meat, tofu, tempe, bean sprouts, noodle, and lettuce

Rawon

Rawon is this dark soup with juicy meat. It is a favourite and popular dish here in Surabaya.

Semanggi

The name Semanggi for this dishes is because the main increadient used in this dishes which is semanggi leaf, a type of plant that is culvatured here in Surabaya. The leaf of semanggi is boiled and then poured with peanut sauce and you scoped it with cracker.
