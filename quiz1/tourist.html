Surabaya Best Tourist Attraction

Surabaya is also offers some of the best and fun tourist attarction there is.

From big and magnificent places to a zoos that have all sort of animals, Surabaya offers a lot to do and enjoy for you!!

Here's some of the places that I recommends you to go if you ever visiting Surabaya.

Tunjungan Plaza Mall

This mall is a very famous Mall in Surabaya, it consist of 6 mall that combined into 1. You can enjoy shopping and culinar in here all day long.

Kebun Binatang Surabaya

Kebun Binatang Surabaya or usually called KBS is a zoo with many kind of animals to see. From big giant elephant to rare small fish, there's always something to see here.

Jalan Tunjungan

Close to Tunjungan Plaza Mall, there's a street that filled with life at night. You can find all sort of street foods and instagramable spot in here. Many teenager comes here to hang around and play with their friends.
