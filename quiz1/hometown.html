My Hometown

My lovely but hotly hometown!

My hometown is in Surabaya Jawa Timur Indonesia

To be precised I lived in the east south of Surabaya, on a housing area of Barata. It is quite nice living there, with all of the neighbour are nive and friendly. We also don't have to go far to get our daily needs like foods and stuff because not far from my house, there's a supermarket and culinary center and many more.

Here's are some fun fact about my hometown that I think you all might like to know about!!

1. It is the 2nd largest city in Indonesia

Surabaya is a metropolitan city with an area of 274.06 km^2. It is become the center of trading and industry business in Jawa Timur province. Many big coorporation have their root company built in here.

2. It is called "Kota Pahlawan"

Surabaya is also called "Kota Pahlawan". The origin of the name is when the colonial period of Indonesia, many peoples from Surabaya fight for their freedom in here and many heroes borned and died here, so to honour their work and sarcrifised, Surabaya gets the nickname "Kota Pahlawan"

3. It is known for the BONEK"

Surabaya is known for their footbal supporter as well, this supporter have a nickname of "BONEK", which stands for "BONDO NEKAT". It is implied that the suppoerter of surabaya will support very deeply and hardly to their team which is called Persebaya
